🔍 Key Business Insights

  • 1 Electronics leads in average order value at ₹5,82,340 in total revenue, driven primarily by high-ticket items like Laptops and Smartphones — indicating strong demand in urban regions. Focused upselling in this category can yield significant margin improvements.
  • 2 February and September are peak revenue months, contributing 11.1% and 10.6% of annual revenue respectively. These spikes align with seasonal sale events — the business should allocate higher inventory and marketing budgets in these months.
  • 3 Returning customers dominate with 36.7% of all orders, followed closely by VIP customers at 35.7%. New customers account for only 27.7%, suggesting a strong retention rate but a need to invest in acquisition campaigns to grow the top-of-funnel.
  • 4 The North region offers the highest average discount (20.89%), which may be eroding profit margins. A pricing audit for this region is recommended to identify whether discounts are translating to proportional volume gains or simply reducing revenue per order.
  • 5 November shows the lowest monthly revenue at ₹1,05,200 — a 70% drop from the February peak. This creates a strategic opportunity to introduce flash sales, bundle offers, or loyalty rewards in Q4 to smoothen the seasonal revenue curve and reduce end-of-year slumps.
